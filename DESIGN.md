# Design System

## Core Aesthetic & Tokens
- **Theme**: Dark, high-contrast B2B SaaS layout with glowing neon accents.
- **Background**: `#050505` deep black with subtle grid overlays and radial light glows.
- **Accent Colors**: `#CCFF00` (Electric Lime), `#00FFFF` (Cyan), `#33FF99` (Mint).
- **Typography**: Inter / Space Grotesk sans-serif with explicit font hierarchies.
- **Borders & Cards**: Glassmorphism cards with `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.05)` borders and subtle hover scale animations.
