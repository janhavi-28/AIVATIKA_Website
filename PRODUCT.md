# Product

<!-- impeccable:product-schema 1 -->

## Platform

web

## Users

B2B and Enterprise organizations seeking automated operations, customer support, and document/invoice processing. Primary decision makers include COOs, CX Leads, and Operations Executives looking to reduce resolution times and scale customer interactions 24/7 without scaling headcount.

## Product Purpose

AIVATIKA provides intelligent AI agents that automate customer conversations (WhatsApp, Web, Email, IG, Slack) and process complex invoices/documents automatically with high accuracy.

## Positioning

Full end-to-end automation engineered with persistent customer memory and multi-channel context. Unlike single-channel bots, AIVATIKA maintains unified customer state across all touchpoints with seamless human-agent handoff.

## Operating Context

- Web marketing site and product showcase
- Interactive simulated agent demos
- High-volume customer support & invoice workflow automation

## Capabilities and Constraints

- **WhatsApp & Omnichannel Bot**: Persistent memory, automated customer support, CRM/Calendar integrations
- **Invoice Automation**: Document parsing, structured extraction, automated validation workflows
- **Zero-Code Deployment**: Fast setup with enterprise-grade security and reliability

## Brand Commitments

- **Name**: AIVATIKA
- **Visual Aesthetic**: Sleek dark mode, neon accents (`#CCFF00`, `#33ff99`), glassmorphism, clean typography (Inter font), grid overlays, dynamic glow effects
- **Tone & Voice**: Authoritative, high-tech, modern, clear, and action-oriented

## Evidence on Hand

- Live interactive demo simulations (`demo.html`)
- Dedicated product landing pages (`chat-automation.html`, `invoice-automation.html`)
- Real-time benchmark claims (e.g. 180ms latency, 94% chat resolution rate)

## Product Principles

1. **Automation First**: Every interaction should feel instant, intelligent, and low-friction.
2. **Context Integrity**: Maintain state and memory across channels so customers never repeat themselves.
3. **High-Tech Craft**: Deliver an award-winning, state-of-the-art visual experience that instills enterprise confidence.
